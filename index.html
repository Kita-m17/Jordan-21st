To Jordy,

Eight years ago, on the very first day of Grade 8, God placed you in my life, and I truly believe that was no accident. I had no idea that day would mark the beginning of the most meaningful friendship I would ever know. You were not just someone I happened to meet. You were sent to me.

You have been my sister, my safe place, my person. Watching you grow into the intelligent, wise, and incredible woman standing here at 21, it fills my heart with a pride I cannot fully describe. Every achievement, every obstacle you overcame, every version of yourself you had to become along the way — I witnessed it all, and I am so proud of you.

I pray that God blesses you in abundance, that every road you take is paved with favour, and that everything you have ever dreamed of finds its way to you. You deserve nothing less than the absolute best this life has to offer.

Here is to you, my sister. Twenty-one looks so good on you. I love you more than I will ever be able to say.
